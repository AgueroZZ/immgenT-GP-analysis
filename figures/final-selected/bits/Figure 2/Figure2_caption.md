# Figure 2. GPs and lineages.

**(A)** Swarm plot of per-GP predictive performance (one-vs-rest AUC, computed on healthy non-thymocyte cells) across the seven major lineages (CD8, CD4, Treg, gdT, CD8aa, Tz, DN). Only up-regulated GPs (mean loading ≥ the overall mean) are plotted as circles; triangles mark forced-highlight GPs (GP3, GP22, GP29) that are down-regulated in gdT, CD8aa, and DN; the dashed line marks AUC = 0.5, and the most predictive GP per lineage is labeled.

**(B)** Structure plot of the membership (GP loading) of six lineage-defining GPs (GP58, GP68, GP30, GP3, GP29, GP22) across cells grouped by major lineages (CD8, CD4, Treg, gdT, CD8aa, Tz, DN); CD4 and CD8 cells are subsampled for visualization.

**(C, E, G)** GP loading projected onto the global UMAP embedding for GP68 (C), GP30 (E), and GP58 (G); each point is a cell, colored by its loading for that GP.

**(D, F, H)** Per-gene view of the same three GPs — GP68 (D), GP30 (F), GP58 (H): each gene's score in the GP (x-axis, scaled so the maximum |score| = 1) versus its mean shifted-log expression (y-axis); top genes are labeled.

**(I)** UMAP restricted to gdT, CD8aa, and DN cells (excluding proliferating and miniverse subsets), colored by lineage.

**(J–L)** GP loading on the same gdT/CD8aa/DN UMAP for GP22 (J), GP29 (K), and GP3 (L).

**(M)** Heatmap of the top 30 gene scores across GP3, GP22, and GP29 (blue, negative; red, positive).
